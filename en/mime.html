Mime

It's happened to everyone...
A night at the movies and you are sitting
next to someone who drives you crazy.
That is the theme of Frères Taquins' parody routine.

Their act is in the old tradition of the ..., and the two comrades cause
us to experience all imaginable emotions,
from laughing to crying – but always joking.

Then like his predecessor Charlie Chaplin, the “goodie” wins in the
end, ... you are always guaranteed a happy end with the Frères Taquins.

This sketch lasts 8 minutes and must be performed on a stage
frontally to the audience.
