Technical

Length of the show

  • Minimum 5 minutes (for the television)
  • 13 minutes (for galas, variety shows, circus)
  • 30 minutes (in theatrical conditions or street shows)

Music

  • Mini-disk or CD
  • Sound volume in accordance with the room-size (95 dba)
  • The technician must face the stage

Scene

  • Minimum 4 metres by 6 metres
  • Plain fabric background cloth if possible
  • Access to the public stalls
  • Maximum dimension : a football pitch

Lighting

  • Minimum :
    1 kw source
  • Ideal :
    On face lighting: 4 colours : white blue 132 red 126
    amber 105
    Tracking: 2 x H M 1 1 200 in the axis, Behind: 3 colours,
    blue 119,
    amber red, 64/22v
